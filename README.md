# Bills-Reminders
Are you tired of scribbling down your pending bill payments on calendars?
Gone are the days when there was no need to memories the dates for your pending payments. As usual, technology has come to rescue you.
Yes, it is now possible to keep track of all your pending bill payments with the help of this app. These apps are designed to be used
on your smart phones and can be used as a reminder for any kind of pending bill payments.
